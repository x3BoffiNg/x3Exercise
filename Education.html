Importance Of Exercising

Benefits Of Exercising


Reduces The Risk Of Heart Diseases. Exercising does NOT only strehthen muscles in our body but also help streghten our heart muscles to become more efficient in blood circulation throughout the body. The increased blood flow increases the oxygen level in the body. This meant that there the chances of contracting heart diseases such as high cholesterol, coronary artery disease, and heart attack is lowered. Researchers found that heart attack patients who participated in a formal exercise program experienced a reduced death rate of 20 to 25 percent.



Controlling & Maintaining Your Weight. Along with diet, exercising plays an important role in maintaining your weight and preventing obesity. To maintain your weight, the calories you intake must be equal to the energy you burn. If you would like to lose weight, the calories intake must be lesser than the energy burned. This can be achieved by being consious about what you eat everyday. The amount of calories a particular food have can be checked on the package itself.



Improves Mental Health. Exercise releases chemicals like endorphins and serotonin that can improve your mood. This can help reduce stress and other mental health such as anxiety and depression.



Builds Stronger Bones. Exercise can help children and teenagers build strong bones and it also essential for maintaining bone strength when we get older. This is because the bone is a living tissue that changes over time in response to the forces placed upon it. Thus when you exercise regularly, your bone adapts by building more bone and becoming denser.
